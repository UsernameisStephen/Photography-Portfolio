About me

Hey, everyone!

My name is Stephen Elliott and I am a freelance Photographer.

I have professional experienece with real estate, auto, portraits, sports and nature landscapes. I also have experience as a writer and videographer.

Media has always had a special place in my heart. Living in Alaska for the first five years of my life, having a camera and camcorder around was a must for my family. I was immediately drawn to the beautiful landscapes we were able to capture in such an amazing place. Because of those memeories, I turned my first passion into professional work.

Something I always try to do with my work is give my clients a bit of that magic feeling I felt from my childhood in each one of my photos and videos.


I love meeting people and taking on new challenges.

Check out my work below and contact me if you would like to work with me!
